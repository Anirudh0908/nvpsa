The Preamble

It is a matter of pride for all of us to be the past student of more than 100 years old outstdanding educational society, which started not only to impart education but to imbibe National values in all of its students. the Nutan Vidyalaya Educational Society has rendered yeoman service in the feild of education during the last nine decades. Over the years the society has gradually developed and many new educational institutions have come up. Past student of any institution have a active role to play in the overall development of its Alma Matter. Our past students are sucessful in thier respective professions since many decades. We have the good fortune to find our past students in all walks of life leading a sucessful carrer and catering to the needs of the commuity where they live in.
